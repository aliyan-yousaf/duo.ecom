Shopify Design & Developers

Who we are

A small studio, run like an in-house team.

DuoEcom started with a simple frustration: most Shopify projects get split across a designer, a developer, and a separate marketing freelancer who never quite talk to each other — and the store suffers for it.

We do it differently. Design, development, and growth all happen under one roof, so nothing gets lost in translation between "how it looks" and "how it sells." You talk directly to the people building your store — no account managers, no middlemen, no guessing who's responsible for what.

Beyond building the store itself, we also run paid marketing campaigns — Meta Ads and Google Ads — so your launch isn't just a good-looking site sitting quietly online. We help put real traffic in front of it from day one and keep optimizing once orders start coming in.

How we think

Three rules we don't break.

01

Design that a developer can actually ship

Every mockup is built knowing exactly how it becomes Liquid — no design that looks great in Figma and breaks at checkout.

02

Speed is a design decision, not an afterthought

We optimize images, scripts, and sections from the first wireframe, not after launch when it's expensive to fix.

03

Mobile is the real homepage

Most of your traffic is on a phone. We design and test mobile-first, then scale up — never the other way around.
